Fun Facts

Creation of Stitch

Stitch, also known as Experiment 626, was created by Dr. Jumba Jookiba, a brilliant but misguided scientist. He was designed to be a perfect criminal with enhanced strength, intelligence, and destructive capabilities.

Genetic Design

Stitch was genetically engineered with DNA from various species, giving him unique abilities like super strength, enhanced hearing, night vision, and the ability to retract his extra arms and antennae.

Transformation

Despite being created for destruction, Stitch's encounter with Lilo and her family changed him. He learned about 'ohana' (family) and discovered that his true purpose was to find a family and protect them.
